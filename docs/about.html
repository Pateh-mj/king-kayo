About the King Kayo Group.

King Kayo Group is a multi-sector conglomerate dedicated to providing specialized industrial, environmental, and logistical solutions across the African landscape.

Public Health, Cleaning & Sanitation.

At the heart of King Kayo Group lies our commitment to a cleaner, safer environment. Our Public Health division specializes in comprehensive medical waste collection and disposal, ensuring that hazardous materials are managed with surgical precision. We recognize that sanitation is the foundation of a thriving society.

From the supply of specialized skip, wheelie, and sanitary bins to advanced landscaping and solid waste transport, we manage the entire environmental lifecycle for our institutional clients.

Transport Solutions

Engineering the Future of Zambia.

Our Engineering and Construction services are built on a bedrock of integrity. We don't just build structures; we build essential community pillars. Our expertise spans the construction of high-capacity hospitals and clinics, modern educational schools, and luxury lodges that redefine hospitality.

To support this vast infrastructure network, our General Supplies division ensures that national projects never stall. We provide everything from mining materials and building components to specialized medical and laboratory equipment, serving as the backbone for regional industrial growth.
