Who We Are

Institutional Profile.

Founded on resilience and excellence, King Kayo Group operates as a diversified institutional powerhouse, bridging the gap between Commercial Delivery and Social Inclusion.

Group Overview

The King Kayo Group is a 100% locally-owned, disability-led conglomerate with a strategic nationwide footprint spanning from Solwezi to Livingstone. By integrating Public Health & Cleaning Services with the heavy industrial engineering of Shumba Trailers & Fabrications, the group delivers end-to-end infrastructure and environmental solutions. This synergy, supported by the procurement expertise of King Kayo General Supplies, allows the group to execute complex projects in waste management, structural steel, and regional supply chains while setting a national benchmark for inclusive economic participation.

Our Mission

Delivering precision-based solutions while championing a diverse, differently-abled workforce to redefine regional industry standards.

Corporate Integrity

We maintain the highest standards of governance and operational transparency, ensuring every project is backed by legal precision.
